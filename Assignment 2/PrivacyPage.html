Privacy

• Welcome to SkillSwap. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

• We collect personal information when you register, create a profile, post skills or requests, and interact with other users. This includes your name, email address, profile details, and any additional information you choose to provide. We may also collect non-personal data such as browser type, device information, and website usage patterns to improve our services.

• Your data is used to create and manage your account, facilitate skill exchanges, process payments if applicable, and enhance your overall experience on our platform. We may also use your information for communication, including service updates, promotional messages, and customer support.
